our values

Curiosity

Embrace a spirit of exploration and openness in discussions. Be eager to discover new insights and willing to adapt as we learn and grow together

Collaboration

Champion the power of teamwork. We believe in combining diverse perspectives to uncover collective solutions that drive us forward.

Civility

Commit to respectful and inclusive communication. We strive to create a welcoming environment where every voice is heard and valued.

get involved

Participate

We occasionally host get-togethers, coworking days, and happy hours. Please join our group on meetup.com/ai-society to see a list of upcoming activities.

Collaborate

The SAIS has attracted local, top-tier AI professionals with specific interests across technology, ethics, sociology, philosophy, political science, economics, and more.

If you're looking for an AI enthusiast to collaborate with on a project (or even just grab coffee!) Please reach out to us directly at hi@seattleAIsociety.org.

Sponsor

The SAIS is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization. We are self-funded by the executive team and supported through donations from sponsors like you.

If you are interested in sponsoring the SAIS, please reach out to us directly at hi@seattleAIsociety.org.

Follow

We would love to stay in touch! You can also follow us on Twitter & LinkedIn, take a peek at our Discourse forum, and of course, register for events at meetup.com/ai-society.

Other

Got another question or inquiry? You can always reach us at hi@seattleAIsociety.org.
